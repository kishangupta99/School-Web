Welcome to St. Chandra Prabha Memorial Academy

St. Chandra Prabha Memorial Academy, located in Pachwas, Basti, Uttar Pradesh, is a co-educational institution affiliated to the Uttar Pradesh Board of Secondary Education. The school is dedicated to providing quality education, fostering holistic development, and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens.

  • Affiliated to Uttar Pradesh Board
  • Co-educational
  • Classes from Nursery to 12th
  • Focus on academics and extracurricular activities
